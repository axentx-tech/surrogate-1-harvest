## Activation triggers (decision framework)

### Activate Path A (employee + share) if:
- [ ] Boss offers 20%+ revenue share within 3-6 months
- [ ] Formal MOU within 4-8 weeks of pitch
- [ ] Budget commitment + 2-3 engineer hire
- [ ] Release planning / hero duties offloaded to new hire
- [ ] Product roadmap + go-to-market budget concrete

### Activate Path E (own company) if:
- [ ] Boss doesn't offer fair term within 3-6 months
- [ ] Day job burnout not improving (work load stable or increasing)
- [ ] Performance review doesn't reflect value delivered
- [ ] Specific client lead ready to pay 5M+ surfaces
- [ ] Personal finance: 6+ months savings runway
- [ ] Family/health OK
- [ ] Energy trending up (not down)

### Stay Path D (shelve) if:
- [ ] Any activation signal missing
- [ ] Energy still low
- [ ] Codebase/vision still maturing
- [ ] Market timing unclear
- [ ] Co-founder Company 1 trauma reactivates

### Red flags — decline all paths if:
- [ ] Health markers degrading (sleep, stress, physical)
- [ ] Family/relationship under strain
- [ ] Finance unstable
- [ ] Company 1 grief reactivates strongly
