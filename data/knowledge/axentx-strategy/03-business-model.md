# axentx Business Model

## Open-Core strategy

| Tier | Product | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **OSS (free)** | Costinel | Bait — get into orgs via FinOps, create pull for Arkship |
| **OSS (free)** | Vanguard | Bait — get into orgs via security, create pull for Arkship |
| **Commercial core** | Arkship | Integrated workflow, orchestration, tier approval |
| **Commercial core** | Surrogate-1 | AI brain, 15 domains, fine-tuned |

**Rationale**: 
- Standalone Costinel/Vanguard = friction (manual tagging, no workflow)
- With Arkship = auto-tag, auto-workflow, massive UX win
- Users who try OSS → hit friction → upgrade to Arkship paid
- Classic GitLab/Elastic/MongoDB play
