# Product Discovery Brief

## Rationale
bd-research outputs across surrogate/Costinel/Vanguard/arkship follow identical structure — market signals, competitor landscape, positioning opportunity, feature suggestions, priority score table. Standardizing ensures consistent depth and comparability across products.

## Steps
1. Pull market/trend excerpts from local RAG index for the product's domain
2. List 3-5 market signals as bullets tied to observable trends
3. Name 3-5 competitors with one-line differentiators
4. Draft 2-3 positioning angles the product can own
5. Score feature suggestions as value(1-5)×feasibility(1-5) and rank
