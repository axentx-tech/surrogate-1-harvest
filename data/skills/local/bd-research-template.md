# Bd Research Template

## Rationale
Pattern appeared 4+ times (surrogate, Costinel x2, Vanguard, arkship) with identical section structure — worth standardizing to ensure consistent depth and scoring.

## Steps
1. Pull market trend excerpts from local RAG index relevant to product domain
2. List 3-5 market signals as bullets with concrete trend evidence
3. Name 3-5 competitors with one-line differentiators each
4. Write 2-3 positioning angles for how product can differentiate
5. Suggest 3-5 features with signal/rationale and score by value×feasibility matrix
