# Bd Research

## Rationale
BD research pattern appeared 4 times with identical structure (Market Signals → Competitors → Positioning → Features → Priority Score); worth standardizing for consistent product intelligence.

## Steps
1. Query RAG index or web for market signals in target domain (3-5 bullets)
2. Identify 3-5 competitors with their differentiators
3. Propose 2-3 positioning angles for differentiation
4. Suggest 3-5 features with signal/rationale
5. Score each feature by value(1-5) × feasibility(1-5) for prioritization
