# Business Market Research

## Rationale
Pattern appeared 4 times across Costinel/arkship research with identical structure—market signals, competitor landscape, positioning, feature suggestions, priority scoring. Domain-general for any product/business analysis.

## Steps
1. Gather market signals from RAG index or web sources (3-5 key trends)
2. Map competitor landscape with differentiators (3-5 competitors)
3. Identify positioning opportunities (2-3 differentiation angles)
4. Generate feature suggestions with signal/rationale (3-5 features)
5. Score features by value×feasibility matrix and rank
